UX Design

Why design matters

In the modern era design plays an important role in how we consume information. Every social media site and advertiser knows this and will use certain methods to convince you to buy or participate in their service. Knowing the ways that designers affect you and what methods me be employed to manipulate you is important to being a well versed consumer in this information era.

About this site

This site will show case a few of the design tricks and methods that websites, products, signs and other mediums employ to affect your behavior or perception of the media you view
