Layout

Layout is the formation of a design. Where the title goes and where the images are, and how big? Where should the body copy go? Imagine if this page had no layout. What would be legible, and what content would be confusing?

Let's look at the layout above; what does it say? You may have to look closer because the layout does not properly organize the content. With a better layout, the order in which you view content becomes clear.
